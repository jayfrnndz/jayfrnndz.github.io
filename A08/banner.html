Black Clover

In the world of Black Clover, magic is everything—status, power, and success all depend on it. Everyone is born with magical abilities... except for Asta, an orphan who has no magic at all. Alongside his childhood friend and rival, Yuno—a gifted magical prodigy—they both share the same dream: to become the Wizard King, the strongest mage in the Clover Kingdom. While Yuno receives a rare four-leaf clover grimoire, Asta unexpectedly obtains a mysterious five-leaf clover grimoire, which grants him anti-magic—a power that can nullify and cut through spells. Despite having no magic, Asta trains his body relentlessly and refuses to give up. Their journey begins when they join the Magic Knights, facing fierce battles, dark enemies, and deep secrets that challenge their beliefs and strength. As they grow stronger, the path to becoming Wizard King becomes clearer—but only one can claim the title. Through it all, Asta is determined to prove that even without magic, he can stand at the top.

Asta

Asta is a loud, energetic, and determined young man born without magic in a world where magic means everything. Armed with a rare five-leaf clover grimoire and anti-magic swords, he overcomes impossible odds through sheer willpower, intense training, and unwavering resolve. Loyal, brave, and never one to back down, Asta is an underdog who strives to become the Wizard King—no matter what stands in his way.
