Mabuhay! Welcome to the Philippines

Discover the islands, the people, and the culture

Islands

Palawan Island

Known for its clear waters, limestone cliffs, and natural wonders like El Nido and the Underground River.

Boracay Island

Famous for its white-sand beach, vibrant nightlife, and water activities.

Siargao Island

A top surf spot with laid-back vibes, clear lagoons, and island-hopping adventures.

People

Tagalog

Found mainly in Luzon; known for hospitality and being the primary speakers of Filipino.

Cebuano

From the Visayas and Mindanao; known for their strong faith and festive culture.

Igorot

Indigenous people of the Cordilleras; known for rice terraces and rich mountain traditions.

Cultures

Harana

A traditional serenade where a man sings love songs to court a woman, often with a guitar.

Fiesta

Colorful town celebrations honoring patron saints, filled with parades, food, and dancing.

Simbang Gabi

A nine-day series of dawn masses leading up to Christmas, showing deep Filipino faith.
